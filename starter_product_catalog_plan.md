# Starter Product Catalog Plan

## Purpose
Define how to create the initial set of known skincare products for SkinIntel.

## Goals
- Provide a small but high-quality starting dataset
- Enable real comparisons and recommendations
- Focus on well-known and dermatologically tested products

## Initial scope
- 20–50 products maximum
- Focus on common categories:
  - cleansers
  - moisturizers
  - acne treatments
  - serums

## Data requirements per product
- brand
- product_name
- category
- inci_raw
- inci_parsed (basic)
- notes (optional)

## Source strategy
- official brand websites
- verified ingredient databases
- trusted skincare resources

## Rules
- prioritize data quality over quantity
- avoid duplicate or incomplete entries
- keep naming consistent

## Future expansion
- admin-added products
- user-submitted products
- automated enrichment over time
